Platform

Against

  • Illegal Immigration

    • Agamemnon is firmly against illegal immigration. However, he also wants to be able to bring in a limited number of legal immigrants in order to elevate the United States’s reputation as a world leader.

  • Gun Control

    • Owning a gun is a man’s right and that it is the most effective defense against terrorist attacks or mass shootings.

  • Obamacare

    • Agamemnon is against the concept of Obamacare. Although it would be ideal to have everyone receive health care, the quality of health care would suffer as a result of this. The government in general should limit its involvement with the lives of citizens, and an exception should not be made for health care. The process of determining a person’s eligibility for such services should be left to those who are better educated about the process, like employers at health insurance companies and physicians themselves.

  • Terrorism

    • Terrorist have declared war against our country and for that they will not remain at peace. Missions will be in place to capture their leaders.

Pro

  • Flat Tax

    • It is unethical to make citizens have to pay more taxes because of their increased success, and that progressive tax acts as a punishment for working hard. A salary is proportional to a person’s success in their field and the time they invest into their careers, and the increase in pay is what rewards people for their hard work.

  • Military

    • Agamemnon advocates better programs for soldier recovery along with programs that help soldiers find work after they have been out of the workforce for so long. Also, he is pushing for more military funding because a strong military is needed for a country that acts as the world’s protector.

  • National Security

    • National Security needs to be strengthed in order to ensure that Americans are safe from terrorism.

  • America

    • Agamemnon's platform is centered around bringing America glory again. Bring back glory, vote for Agamemnon!
